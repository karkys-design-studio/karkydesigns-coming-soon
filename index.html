In an era where childhood is increasingly consumed by digital screens, Karky Designs, an IIT Kanpur-incubated startup, has launched a vital mission to reconnect children with nature. As climate change and global instability make the future increasingly unpredictable, traditional rote learning is no longer enough to prepare the next generation. Bridging this gap, our team of IIT alumni has developed India’s first ‘Green Skills Simulator.’ This NEP 2020-aligned innovation transforms confined indoor hours into screen-free, skill-building experiences, empowering the youth to build the foundation for a sustainable and Atmanirbhar Bharat, and a sustainable world.
